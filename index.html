about_suvi.txt

Success without sovereignty is a cage.

I built a career inside systems that weren't designed to fully hold me — the fame, the visibility, the validation. Then I stepped away. Not because I failed, but because I refused to disappear inside someone else's vision.

In 2025 I came home to Chennai after fifteen years in the U.S. — not with nostalgia, but with responsibility. Today I stand as a singer, songwriter, rapper, DJ, producer, healer, mother, and wife, building all of it in the open.

My story isn't theoretical. It's twenty years lived. And it's proof that spiritual and strategic, soft and formidable, creative and financially sovereign are not opposites — they're the blueprint.
